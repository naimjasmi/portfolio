About Me

Hello there! I'm a 23-year-old enthusiast born in the charming town of Teluk Intan, Perak, and currently residing in vibrant Shah Alam, Selangor. My journey in the realm of computer science began at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), where I pursued both my Diploma and ongoing Degree, fueled by a passion for technology and innovation. Alongside my academic pursuits, I've cultivated a love for creative expression, finding joy in sketching, digital drawing, and imaginative thinking. When I'm not immersed in the digital realm, I'm out on the badminton or football court, embracing the thrill of sports and staying active. Photography is another avenue through which I unleash my creativity, capturing life's moments with precision and flair. With a knack for editing, I transform ordinary images into captivating masterpieces. My diverse interests and experiences shape my holistic approach to life, allowing me to thrive in both the realms of technology and creativity.
