Projects

Facial Emotion Recognition System For School Counsellor

The "Facial Emotion Recognition System for School Counsellor" is a web application that utilizes advanced technology to analyze facial expressions of students in real-time. By capturing images through a webcam or uploaded photos, the system employs Python, Flask, TensorFlow, and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to detect and recognize various emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, fear, and surprise. The user interface, developed using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, provides an intuitive platform for school counsellors to interact with the system and gain insights into the emotional well-being of students. This project aims to equip counsellors with valuable tools to support students' emotional needs and foster a positive school environment.

Car Service Booking System

The "Car Service Booking System" is a mobile application designed to streamline the process of booking car services for users. Developed using Java and Prestige in Android Studio, this application offers a user-friendly interface for customers to easily schedule appointments for car maintenance, repairs, or other services. With intuitive features and functionalities, users can select their preferred service, choose a convenient date and time, and even specify any additional requirements. By leveraging the power of mobile technology, this app aims to enhance the customer experience by providing a convenient and efficient way to manage car servicing needs on the go.

Car Service Booking Website

The "Car Service Booking Website" is a web application designed to simplify the process of scheduling car services online. Developed using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL, this platform offers users an intuitive interface to book appointments for car maintenance, repairs, or other services. With responsive design and interactive features, customers can easily navigate the website, select their desired service, choose a suitable date and time, and provide any necessary details. The backend functionality, powered by PHP and SQL, ensures seamless booking management and data storage. By providing a user-friendly and efficient booking experience, this website aims to enhance customer satisfaction and streamline the car service booking process.
